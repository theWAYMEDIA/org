## Welcome to Wayism

### What is Wayism?

Wayism is an ancient existential philosophy recognizing that **all humans** are on a sacred journey. We call this the *Butterfly Path* because, like a caterpillar transforms into a butterfly, human souls evolve into immortal spiritual beings -- this is the purpose and meaning of life.

This is not metaphor‚Äîit describes the literal purpose of human existence: souls incarnate as humans to attend Earth's school of divinity, learning through direct experience to eventually graduate as immortal spiritual beings capable of cosmic (more like galactic) service.
