## Wayist Insight

This is the flowing German devotional version of the "All Are OM" teaching — designed for community singing rather than line-by-line instruction. Where the full teaching dharani walks through each theological concept literally, this version captures the essence in singable, poetic German.

The key teachings are all present: universal energy pervading all things, consciousness woven through space and time, the So Ham recognition ("I Am That"), the naming of both Father God and Mother God in heaven, and the communal declaration "Wir sind Energie, alle sind Aum" — we are energy, all are OM.

"Lebendige Nachbarn, nah und weit" (living neighbours, near and far) carries the Wayist understanding that all beings around us are conscious energy — neighbours in the deepest sense. And "Geistwesen, Licht und Kraft" (spirit-beings, light and power) names what we are: not bodies with spirits, but spirit-beings composed of light and energy.

For the full theological commentary on this teaching, see the [Om Mārge Sarva Om teaching dharani](/lyrics/om-marge-sarva-om-german/).
