## Wayist Insight

Esta es la versión devocional en español de la enseñanza "Todos Somos OM" — diseñada para el canto comunitario. Donde la dharani de enseñanza completa recorre cada concepto teológico literalmente, esta versión captura la esencia en español poético y cantable.

This is the flowing Spanish devotional version of the "All Are OM" teaching. All the key Wayist teachings are present in singable form: universal energy pervading all things, consciousness alive all around us, the So Ham declaration, and both divine parents named together in heaven.

"Vecinos vivos en derredor" (living neighbours all around) expresses the Wayist understanding that every being surrounding us is alive with consciousness — neighbour is not a moral category but an ontological reality. "Seres de espíritu y luz" (beings of spirit and light) names our true nature.

For the full theological commentary, see the [Om Mārge Sarva Om teaching dharani](/lyrics/om-marge-sarva-om-spanish/).
