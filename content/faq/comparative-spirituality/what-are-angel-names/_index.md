A: Yes, graduated spiritual beings exist in Sukhavati Heaven serving various functions. Whether they use those specific Hebrew names is doubtful (no mouths, remember?). Those are human cultural identifiers for beings with actual energy signatures.

**Q: How do I know which angel is my Divine Tara?**
A: You don't need to know. Your Tara knows you. Focus on developing that relationship, not on identifying which cataloged angel it might correspond to in human hierarchical systems.

**Q: Should I stop using angel names entirely?**
A: Use whatever helps your connection. If "Archangel Michael" resonates and helps you connect, fine. Just don't think the name itself has power, or that wrong name means no connection. The relationship matters, not the label.

**Q: Are you saying all angel books and teachings are wrong?**
A: We're saying they're often human organizational attempts to catalog spiritual reality. Some wisdom may exist in them, but fixating on names and hierarchies distracts from actual relationship development. Take what helps connection; leave what creates anxiety or dependency.
