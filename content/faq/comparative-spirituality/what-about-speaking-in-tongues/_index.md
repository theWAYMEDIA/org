## Understanding "Speaking in Tongues"

### What Is Actually Happening

**The Phenomenon Described:**
- Person enters altered state
- Makes unintelligible sounds
- Sounds follow no linguistic pattern
- Cannot be decoded by linguists
- Person claims not to understand what they're saying
- Sometimes another person "interprets" (also unprovably)
- Often occurs in group settings with social pressure

**The Lower-Self Reality:**
This is disembodied beings (not Sukhavati spiritual beings) temporarily affecting lower-self faculties:
- Creating sounds through your vocal apparatus
- Bypassing conscious understanding
- Operating at material energy level (sound vibrations)
- Not providing wisdom or guidance
- Not serving your conscious development

**What It's NOT:**
- Authentic communication from Divine Tara
- Proof of Holy Spirit presence
- Spiritual advancement indicator
- Biblical "gift of languages" (actual languages)
- Higher consciousness experience

### Why This Differs from Authentic Inspiration

**Authentic Inspiration (En-theos = God Within):**
- You remain fully conscious and aware
- Understanding comes WITH the experience
- Wisdom serves your development
- You can explain what you learned
- Happens at higher-self level
- Increases consciousness

**Tongues Phenomenon:**
- Consciousness diminished or bypassed
- No understanding accompanies sounds
- No wisdom results
- Cannot explain what occurred
- Happens at lower-self level
- Decreases conscious awareness

## The Biblical "Gift of Tongues" Was Different

### What Scripture Actually Described

**Acts 2 (Pentecost Event):**
People spoke in actual languages they didn't know:
- Hearers each understood in their own language
- Real linguistic communication occurred
- Everyone knew what was being said
- Served practical purpose (spreading teaching to multiple language groups)
- Clear, understandable message

**This Is NOT Modern Tongues:**
- Actual languages (Parthian, Mede, Elamite, etc. named)
- Understood by native speakers
- Verified by hearers
- Purposeful communication
- Not unintelligible sounds

**Modern Tongues Claims:**
- Unintelligible sounds
- No native speakers understand
- Cannot be linguistically verified
- Requires unprovable "interpretation"
- No clear communication occurs

**The Honest Recognition:**
Whatever modern "speaking in tongues" is, it's not the biblical phenomenon described in Acts. That was miraculous language ability for spreading teaching across language barriers. Modern tongues bypass understanding entirely.

**But Still Not Authentic:**
Genuine spiritual communication doesn't require group pressure, doesn't bypass consciousness, and serves clear developmental purpose.
