# Who Is Jesus in Wayism?

## The Foundation Problem

**Christianity teaches Jesus as exclusive divine savior requiring belief for salvation, while simultaneously obscuring his original teaching of The Way through theological overlay and mistranslation** that serves institutional control rather than spiritual development. This creates a double barrier: you must believe specific doctrines about Jesus's identity while being prevented from understanding what he actually taught.

**Teaching vs. Theology Recognition:** *"When you read Jesus's actual words - 'walk as I walk,' 'be perfect as your Father in heaven is perfect,' 'the kingdom of God is within you' - do these sound like someone asking for belief about his identity, or someone teaching a path of transformation?"*

**Historical Context Examination:** *"Jesus was Jewish, taught in Aramaic, and his followers were called 'People of The Way' for decades before 'Christianity' emerged. What might his teaching have been before Greek philosophy and Roman politics shaped it into a religion about him rather than a path taught by him?"*

## Jesus and "Son of God"

### Understanding the Original Meaning

**The Translation Problem:**
Ancient Greek manuscripts were written in all capitals with no spaces between words - there was no way to distinguish "son of god" from "Son of God." Later translators added capitalization to inject theological claims that weren't in the original text.

**The Jewish Context:**
Psalm 82:6 states: "I said, 'You are gods; you are all sons of the Most High.'" This reflects ancient Wayist understanding that came through Melchizedek to Jewish mystical tradition - we are ALL children of God, developing toward spiritual maturity.

**Jesus's Teaching:**
When Jesus spoke of being "son of God," he taught that we are all children of our Father in Heaven. The exclusive "Son of God" title is a later Christian invention that contradicts his actual teaching about universal divine parentage.

**The Anahata Teaching:**
Jesus taught about the seed of spirit from God the Father nurtured by God our Mother in Heaven. Through the Butterfly Path, we metamorphose from soul-beings into spiritual beings - complete transformation like caterpillar to butterfly. In this sense, graduated spirits are truly "children" (offspring) of the divine parents in Heaven.

## The "Only Begotten Son" Claim

### Where This Teaching Originated

**Not Jesus's Teaching:**
This is much later Christian theology that does not apply to People of The Way or Wayism.

**The Possible Source:**
The Lotus Sutra (predating Christianity by hundreds of years) explains that Avalokiteshvara and his sister Prajnaparamita were the first spirit beings created by Amitabha (God the Father) and PandaraJanani (God the Mother) when they established Sukhavati Heaven.

**"Begotten" Context:**
Because they were created directly by the deities rather than through soul-to-spirit evolution, they are described as "begotten" - meaning unborn, not the result of biological procreation or educational graduation.

**The Misunderstanding:**
Christianity likely encountered this teaching and misapplied it, creating exclusive claims about Jesus that contradict both his original teaching and the broader spiritual reality of many graduated spirits in Heaven.

## Jesus as "Saviour of the World"

**Buddhist Teaching Predating Christianity:**
